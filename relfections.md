# Portfolio Reflection

## What I Learned
Over the course of the bootcamp, I gained hands-on experience across the full stack of modern software engineering. I learned how to design, build, and deploy applications that are cloud-ready, secure, and scalable. Key lessons included:
- **Cloud deployments**: Using Firebase and containerization to deliver production-ready workloads.
- **AI/ML integration**: Experimenting with LLM demos and exploring hybrid ML/DL project ideas.
- **DevOps practices**: Building CI/CD pipelines, automating deployments, and managing secrets securely.
